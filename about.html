Why We Built This For Faridabad

In a fast-growing city like Faridabad, it’s easy to feel lost in the crowd. People move to NIT or Sector 21 for work or studies, and often find it hard to make genuine, platonic connections.

That's why we created Walk With Me Faridabad. We realized that there's a huge gap—people want to hang out, they just don't know who to ask. This platform bridges that gap. It's not a dating app; it's a strictly platonic, community-driven space to find someone with shared interests.

Our Mission

To eradicate urban loneliness in Faridabad by providing a safe, accessible, and completely free platform for locals to meet and form meaningful friendships.

Our Vision

We envision a tightly-knit Faridabad where nobody has to walk in Town Park alone or sip coffee at Crown Interiorz by themselves unless they choose to.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide this community initiative.

100% Free Forever

Friendship shouldn't be transactionary. Our service is completely free, and there are no premium tiers or hidden charges.

Safety & Verification

We connect users via a moderated system and emphasize public, safe meeting places only.

Local First

Designed exclusively for Faridabad. We know the pulse of the city, from Old Faridabad to the developing sectors.

Inclusivity

Everyone is welcome. Regardless of age, gender, or background, there's a friend for you here.
