Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hanging out safely.

Is it actually free?
Yes. 100%. We do not charge anything for listing your profile or for connecting with others. The only cost you incur is what you actually consume during your hangout (like your own coffee or movie ticket).
Is this a dating service?
No. While you are free to form whatever bonds you wish, Walk With Me Faridabad is officially a strictly platonic service aimed at fostering community friendship and combatting loneliness.
How do you ensure safety?
We highly recommend meeting ONLY in public places (malls, busy cafes, parks during daylight). Do not share your home address. If a user acts inappropriately, report them and they will be banned from the platform.
Who pays for the activities?
We advocate for "Dutch" — everyone pays for themselves. However, you can discuss this mutually before the hangout via WhatsApp.
